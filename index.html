Our Story

Four decades of delicious memories

A Long Beach Tradition

Since 1984, Corral Drive-In has been serving up authentic American comfort food to the Long Beach community and visitors from around the world. What started as a simple drive-in has become a beloved local institution, known for our legendary Tsunami Burger and warm, family-friendly atmosphere.

Our commitment to quality ingredients, generous portions, and genuine hospitality has kept customers coming back for nearly 40 years. From our signature burgers to our crispy fries and refreshing milkshakes, every item on our menu is prepared with care and pride.

Tsunami Challenge

Can You Handle the Tsunami?

Our legendary Tsunami Burger is not for the faint of heart! This massive burger includes:

  • 1/2 pound fresh beef patty
  • Double bacon
  • Special Tsunami sauce
  • All the fixings
  • Side of crispy fries

Rules: Finish the entire burger and fries in 30 minutes to join our Wall of Fame!

Warning: This challenge is not recommended for those with heart conditions or dietary restrictions.
